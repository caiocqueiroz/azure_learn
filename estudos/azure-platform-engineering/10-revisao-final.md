# Revisão final

## Frases úteis para memorizar

- “The main trade-off is control versus operational overhead.”
- “I prefer guardrails over gates.”
- “I would start with a progressive migration.”
- “Git should be the source of truth.”
- “The artifact should be immutable.”
- “Cost is an architectural signal.”
- “Security should be built into the platform, not added later.”
- “The goal is to create a paved road for engineering teams.”
- “I would classify workloads by criticality before choosing the architecture.”
- “If I don’t need full Kubernetes control, I don’t want to pay the operational cost of full Kubernetes.”

## Prioridade final de estudo

Se o tempo estiver curto, priorize:

1. ACA vs AKS vs App Service;
2. Private Endpoint vs Service Endpoint;
3. Como expor ACA com App Gateway/APIM;
4. SQL VM → SQL MI vs Azure SQL DB;
5. Blue/green com revisions;
6. GitOps e CI/CD;
7. Managed Identity + Key Vault;
8. FinOps;
9. Terraform enterprise structure;
10. Histórias reais com contexto, decisão, trade-off e resultado.

## Posicionamento final

Use esta ideia como seu norte:

> “I’m a pragmatic platform/cloud architect who has worked with real production constraints: governance, networking, CI/CD, cost, security, migrations and operational maturity. I don’t try to apply one technology everywhere. I evaluate trade-offs and build platforms that help teams deliver safely.”
